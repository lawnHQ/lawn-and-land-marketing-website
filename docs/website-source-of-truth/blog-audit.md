# Blog Audit & Migration Determination

Audited all **81 posts** from `lawnandlandmarketing.com/post-sitemap.xml` (2026-06-23), each read on its
actual content by a reviewer. Verdict: **7 migrate · 12 borderline (rewrite-or-cut) · 62 cut.**

**The pattern:** the blog is overwhelmingly AI bulk-filler. The oldest posts (2023 cornerstone guides) are
the strongest — clearly human-written. The huge 2024 batch (published in same-day clusters of 7–10) and the
2026 "client growth story" posts (templated Mow Money podcast recaps) are the weak ones. Tell-tale signs
across the cut set: one templated skeleton (intro → sections → Final Thoughts → Key Takeaways → boilerplate
FAQ → promo CTA), forced garden puns, no named tools / numbers / real examples, heavy near-duplication, and
some written for the wrong audience (homeowner lawn-care tips instead of marketing advice for owners).
