How is landscaping SEO different from lawn care SEO?

They share the same foundation but target different work. Lawn care SEO is built around recurring treatment programs like fertilization and weed control, where one customer renews season after season. Landscaping SEO targets the broader mix you take on, design, installs, plantings, and cleanups, where a single search can turn into a large project plus repeat work. We optimize for the way homeowners actually search for landscaping, not the way a generalist agency would.

How long before landscaping SEO starts bringing in projects?

It depends on your market: population, competition, keyword difficulty, and the momentum you already have. SEO is the slowest part of your marketing, but over time it becomes the most effective and the hardest for competitors to take from you. As a general range, expect 6 to 12 months before organic search is a steady source of landscaping estimates, with smaller wins along the way.

Can you rank my landscaping company in every town I serve?

Yes. We build out your site and local presence for every town in your service area, not just the city your shop sits in, so you show up across your whole footprint instead of leaving nearby markets to competitors.

Do I need a new website, or can you optimize the one I have?

For SEO to really work it needs the right foundation, which is why we build new. We don't patch existing sites, because the problems are usually buried in the code where they quietly hold rankings back. Our from-scratch builds are made to rank and to keep adapting as search and AI change.

How do you prove the SEO is actually generating estimates?

We watch impressions, clicks, and rankings climb in Google Search Console and Analytics, then tie it to real results: form submissions and phone calls tracked through a dedicated number on your site, so you see actual estimate requests, not just traffic.
