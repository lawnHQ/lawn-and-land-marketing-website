About the show

By owners, for owners.

Mow Money, Mow Problems is a weekly podcast for green-industry business owners. Every episode is a real, unscripted conversation with an owner who is in it every day, from first-season startups to seven-figure operations, about how they actually run and grow the business.

It is hosted by Matt Foreman, founder of Lawn & Land Marketing and author of the book the show is named after. No fluff, no theory, just the trials, triumphs, and lessons of building a green-industry company.
